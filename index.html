Severus Snake

The player controls a long, slender creature that resembles a snake as it moves around on a plane with borders, gathering food (or other items), while attempting to avoid slamming into its own tail or the edges of the playing area. The snake's tail lengthens as it consumes food, and for each level the movement of the snake will be more faster, which makes the game harder to play. The game mode can be selected by the player from the two options, CLASSIC MODE and MODERN MODE.

Moseo De Lokal

Moseo De Lokal is an innovative mobile application designed to promote and showcase the diverse talent of local artists within a community. The app aims to bridge the gap between artists and art enthusiasts by providing a user-friendly platform for artists to showcase their artworks and connect with a broader audience. By facilitating this connection, the app seeks to foster a stronger appreciation for local art and contribute to the growth of the local scene.
